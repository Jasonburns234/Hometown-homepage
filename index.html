Visit Cumbria

Enjoy The Lake District and Cumbria

The bustling small towns of Ambleside on Windermere lake and Keswick are particularly popular with visitors.

Ambleside

In the summer months you can take a horse drawn carriage from the centre of Ambleside for the 1 mile to Waterhead Pier.

Windermere

Windermere lake, at 10.5 miles long, one mile wide and 220 feet deep, is the largest natural lake in both the Lake District and in England.

Keswick

Keswick, situated between the huge bulk of Skiddaw and the gentle beauty of Derwentwater, has become the major centre for tourism in the northern Lake District.
